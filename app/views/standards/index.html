Published standards

These standards are published and must be met as part of the product delivery lifecycle.

Standard Title Summary
DDTS-301 Digital Product Branding The Digital Product Branding standard details the requirements for how and when branding should be used.
DDTS-529 Plain Language Ensures digital content is clear, concise, and accessible to all users, including those with lower literacy levels and cognitive impairments.
DDTS-34 Accessibility Statements The accessibility statements standard ensures products and websites meet accessibility regulations.
DDTS-463 Accessibility Conformance The accessibility conformance standard outlines requirements for ensuring digital services meet WCAG guidelines and other accessibility regulations.
