{% markdown %} The new [Digital Product Branding standard](https://standards.education.gov.uk/standard/digital-product-branding) is now available on the Find and Use Standards product. This standard provide clear guidance on the consistent use of the DfE's visual identity across all digital products. The standard is designed to ensure that all products, both internal and externally-facing, adhere to the department's visual identity. The standard includes usage of the DfE logo, typography, and other design elements that reflect the DfE's commitment to clarity, accessibility, and consistency. ## A step towards greater consistency The publication of the standard is part of a wider initiative to strengthen design and accessibility standards across the department. By standardising the use of branding, we create a more recognisable identity that builds trust with users and enhances the clarity of the products we build. ## Guidelines for all teams Available now to all DfE teams and external partners, the standard provides detailed guidelines that can be accessed through the [Find and use standards](https://standards.education.gov.uk) product. This centralised location for all DfE standards is designed to enable staff and external collaborators to deliver high-quality, consistent outputs across various projects and services. Guidance for using the DfE logo, colour palettes, typography, and other design elements can be found in the [DfE design system](/design-system). {% endmarkdown %}
