Theme 1 - Communities of practice

Developing what it means to have a community of practice for design professions.

How we encourage people to join our communities and contribute.

How we develop mature communities.

Theme 2 - People and objectives

What are our professional objectives across design and how we measure them consistently.

How we onboard new people joining design professions.

How we develop peoples skills and capability.

Theme 3 - Career Pathways

What does it look like to change profession within design or move into a design profession.

Identifying skills and training opportunities.

Theme 4 - Patterns and Components

Creating a common component library for DfE specific components to reuse across services.

Publishing the patterns in services that enable us to design consistent services for our users.

Make it easy to find and use patterns and contribute research and insight on patterns.

Theme 5 - Content Styles

The language we use in services is important. How do we ensure this is consisitent, easy to understand, and uses plain English.

How do we ensure we explain complicated things easily, and in an accessible way.

Theme 6 - Measuring design

Recognise we need to understand how our services work for our users.

Creating a base set of design metrics for all services to measure against.

Demonstrate what good design looks like and share good practice.

Theme 7 - Accessibility

How we ensure accessibility is at the core of everything we design.

Developing accessibility testing and awareness skills and capability.

Theme 8 - Design maturity

Working across professions and the department to develop and grow design maturity.
