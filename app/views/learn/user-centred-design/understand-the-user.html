What UCD is

UCD process

  • Understand the user
  • Iterate and improve

Techniques

User-centered design starts with a comprehensive understanding of the user, who is at the heart of the process.

User research

Conduct interviews, surveys, and observations to gather qualitative and quantitative data about users.

Create personas

Create user personas to demonstrate common traits, behaviors, and needs among user groups.

Empathy mapping

Build empathy for your users by putting yourself in their shoes to understand their behaviour and experiences.

User journey mapping

Map out the steps a user takes to complete a task, identifying the pain points and opportunities for improvement.

Understand context

Understand the situation in which the user will be interacting with your service or product. This context could include physical, real-world situations or the technology they use.
