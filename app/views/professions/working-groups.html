Professions {% markdown %} # {{ title }} {{description}} ## Patterns and components Developing consistent design components and patterns for digital services in DfE The working group consists of a cross-profession group of people from design, content, and development. The group meets regularly to discuss and agree on design patterns and components for the department. You can find out what we're working on in the [patterns](/design-system/patterns) and [components](/design-system/components) sections of the design system. Get in touch through the [patterns and components Slack channel (DfE staff only)](https://ukgovernmentdfe.slack.com/archives/C04JZ5YL8F7). ## Content style working group The content style working group develop and maintain the [style guide](/content-design/style-guide) for DfE Get in touch through the [content style group Slack channel (DfE staff only)](https://ukgovernmentdfe.slack.com/archives/C04JZ5YL8F7). ## Service mapping This is a cross-profession, cross-department project to build a living map of products and services across DfE to identify opportunities for collaboration and sharing. Get in touch with the [Head of Design](/designops) or contact the wider group in the [service mapping Slack channel (DfE staff only)](https://ukgovernmentdfe.slack.com/archives/C04JZ5YL8F7). {% endmarkdown %}
