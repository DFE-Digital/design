Professions {% markdown %} # {{ title }} {{description}} ## Patterns and components Developing consistent design components and patterns for digital services in DfE The working group consists of a cross-profession group of people from design, content, and development. The group meets regularly to discuss and agree on design patterns and components for the department. You can find out what we're working on in the [patterns](/design-system/patterns) and [components](/design-system/components) sections of the design system.
