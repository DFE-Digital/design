Summary

Overall, in 2022, The results showed a mixed level of maturity in design-related aspects, with room for improvement in various areas, including setting specific design goals, increasing leadership support, and establishing consistent design processes and career development paths.

Results

The average maturity score was 3.2 out of 6.

Notes on results data

  1. This was the first year the survey was run, so there is no comparable data.
  2. The percentages in the table represent the proportion of respondents who selected each response category.
