Initiatives

Some of the initiatives we're working on to support this priority.

Raising standards across all DfE services

Focus on enhancing the quality and consistency of all services provided by the Department for Education.

This involves setting standards for design and delivery to ensure that each product and service meets the department's goals and user expectations.

Enabling designers to create consistent and accessible products

Provide designers with the tools, resources, and guidance needed to design products and services that are both consistent and accessible.

Creating accessible services for users

Ensure that all products and services designed are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.

We are committed to embedding accessibility in every stage of the design and development process, from policy through to delivery and beyond.
