We are a team who lead on design and accessibility for design acoss the department and support the following professions.

  • content design
  • interaction design
  • service design

This is around 150 people across the department.

What we do

We enable designers to do their job, through:

  • organising and identifying learning, development and training
  • setting standards for good design practices
  • identifying and publishing common components and patterns
  • creating tools and services to improve design processes
  • raising awareness and understanding of design and accessibility in DfE

We work in the open. You can read our priorities which help us work on the right problems.
