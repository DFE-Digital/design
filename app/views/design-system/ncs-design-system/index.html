You should use the National Careers Service design system to help you design and create pages on the National Careers Service website. They cannot be used on any other websites or products.

This will help ensure we design a consistent service for users accessing the National Careers Service.

GOV.UK and DfE Design System

The National Careers Service website is hosted on a service.gov.uk domain and is managed by a team within the Department for Education.

This means that for anything that does not exist in the National Careers Service design system, you can use the GOV.UK Design System and the main DfE Design Manual.
