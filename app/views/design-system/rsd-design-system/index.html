You should use the Regional Services Division design system to help you design and create applications to support RSD.Regional Service Division design histories website. They cannot be used on any other websites or products.

This will help ensure we design a consistent service for users accessing the RSD Services.

GOV.UK and DfE Design System

Regional Services Division applications and websites are hosted on gov.uk domains and are managed by a team within the Department for Education.

This means that for anything that does not exist in the Regional Services Division design system, you can use the GOV.UK Design System and the main DfE Design Manual.
